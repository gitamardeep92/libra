FAQ

Got Questions?

Is my data safe? Where is it stored? ↓
Your data is stored on enterprise-grade servers with daily automatic backups. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your data with anyone. Even if you cancel, your data is kept safely for 30 days before permanent deletion.
Can I use it on my mobile phone? ↓
Yes! LibraryDesk is fully responsive and works on any device — desktop, tablet or mobile. No app download needed. Just open your browser and go to app.librarydesk.in. It works perfectly on Chrome, Safari and any modern browser.
How many students and seats can I add? ↓
Unlimited. There are no per-student or per-seat charges. Whether you have 50 students or 500 students, the price is the same flat ₹1000/month. We believe in simple, honest pricing.
What payment methods can I record for my students? ↓
You can record payments made via Cash, UPI, Card, Net Banking, Cheque and more. LibraryDesk tracks payment mode per subscription so your accounts are always accurate. We currently support manual payment recording with Razorpay online payment coming soon.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ↓
Absolutely. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your data remains accessible until the end of your billing period. We think you'll love LibraryDesk, but we never hold you hostage.
Do you offer support in Hindi? ↓
Yes! Our support team is fluent in Hindi and English. You can reach us on WhatsApp, email or through the contact form on this page. We typically respond within a few hours on working days.
What happens after my 14-day trial ends? ↓
After the trial, your account switches to read-only mode — you can still view all your data but won't be able to add new students or subscriptions. Simply upgrade to the Monthly or Annual plan to continue with full access. Your data is never deleted.
